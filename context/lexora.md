# Lexora - AI Legal Assistant

## Product Overview

**Lexora** is an AI-powered legal research assistant that provides multilingual, cross-jurisdictional search and analysis of administrative law across Montenegro, Serbia, and Croatia. Built by Codeus, Lexora leverages state-of-the-art Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology to help legal professionals, judges, researchers, and citizens access comprehensive legal information quickly and accurately.

## What is Lexora?

Lexora is a multilingual RAG-based legal search system that enables natural language queries across a comprehensive database of administrative law. It represents a technological breakthrough by combining:

- **Comprehensive Coverage:** 1M+ administrative court decisions and regulations
- **Multi-jurisdictional:** Montenegro, Serbia, and Croatia
- **Multilingual:** English, Montenegrin, Serbian, and Croatian
- **Advanced AI:** RAG technology for accurate, contextual results
- **High Performance:** Target <6 second response time, >85% accuracy, <10% hallucination rate

## Core Technology

### RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
Lexora uses RAG technology, which combines:
1. **Semantic Retrieval:** Finding relevant legal documents based on meaning, not just keywords
2. **Context-Aware Generation:** Producing accurate answers grounded in retrieved documents
3. **Multilingual Embeddings:** Understanding queries across different languages
4. **Fine-tuning:** Specialized optimization for legal domain

### Performance Targets
- **Accuracy:** >85% (outperforming leading solutions like Lexis+AI at ~65%)
- **Hallucination Rate:** <10% (vs. 17-33% for competitors)
- **Response Time:** <6 seconds (vs. 6-15+ seconds for competitors)
- **Coverage:** 100% of administrative law corpus since 2015 in all three jurisdictions

## Key Features

### 1. Multilingual Legal Search
- Natural language queries in 4 languages: English, Montenegrin, Serbian, Croatian
- Semantic search that understands legal concepts, not just keywords
- Cross-language search capability (query in one language, find results in another)

### 2. Cross-Jurisdictional Comparative Analysis
**First in the Western Balkans**
- Automatically identify similar cases across Montenegro, Serbia, and Croatia
- Compare legal interpretations and outcomes between jurisdictions
- Identify jurisdictional divergences and common practices
- Essential for multinational corporations and comparative legal research

### 3. Statistical Querying and Analytics
**Unique in the market**
- Natural language queries over legal data patterns
- Find success rates for specific case types
- Identify judicial trends and asymmetries
- Predict litigation outcomes based on historical data
- Examples:
  - "What percentage of appeals succeed in Montenegro vs. Serbia?"
  - "Show me conviction rate trends for the past 5 years"
  - "How many cases ruled in favor of plaintiffs in similar circumstances?"

### 4. Multi-Level Case Summarization
- **Executive Summary:** Quick overview for rapid assessment
- **Detailed Summary:** Key facts, legal issues, and reasoning
- **Comprehensive Summary:** Full analysis with citation traceability
- Automatic extraction of key facts, legal issues, holdings, and rationale
- Full citation traceability to source documents

### 5. Precedent Chain Mapping
- Visualize how cases cite and build upon each other
- Track evolution of legal interpretations over time
- Identify foundational cases in specific legal areas

## Target Users

### For Judges
**Value Proposition:** Quickly compare rulings across courts and reduce decision-making time
- Research similar cases from other jurisdictions for reasoning support
- Access comprehensive precedent analysis instantly
- Reduce time spent on routine case research from days to minutes
- Ensure consistency with established jurisprudence

**Use Case:** A judge in Podgorica handling an administrative dispute can instantly find similar cases from Belgrade and Zagreb courts, compare reasoning, and identify best practices - a task that previously took days of manual research.

### For Legal Professionals (Lawyers, Attorneys)
**Value Proposition:** Build stronger cases with comprehensive precedent analysis
- Assess case viability before taking it on
- Find supporting precedents across three jurisdictions
- Identify successful legal strategies from similar cases
- Prepare more effective arguments with statistical backing

**Use Case:** A lawyer can query "success rate of appeals against tax authority decisions in the last 3 years" to advise clients whether to proceed with litigation or seek settlement.

### For Researchers & Law Professors
**Value Proposition:** Access the most complete administrative law database in the region
- Conduct comparative legal studies efficiently
- Identify trends and patterns in judicial decision-making
- Create case studies for teaching from multiple jurisdictions
- Publish research based on comprehensive data analysis

**Use Case:** A law professor preparing a lecture on administrative procedure can instantly locate and compare examples from all three jurisdictions, condensed into teaching materials.

### For Citizens
**Value Proposition:** Understand your legal situation in plain language
- Get clear explanations of relevant laws and precedents
- Understand likelihood of success before engaging a lawyer
- Make informed decisions about pursuing legal action
- Access justice information without legal expertise

**Use Case:** A citizen facing an administrative dispute can ask in plain language "What happens if I appeal a building permit denial?" and receive relevant precedents and success rates.
